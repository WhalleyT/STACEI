TCR Contact Maps

TCR contact maps present contact data between TCR residues (top row) and peptide/MHC residues (bottom row) by connecting residues with lines indicating that a contact between these residues is present. Contact lines are coloured by contact type that is present: vdW contacts are solid grey, SB are dashed red and HB are blue dashed. Contacts between residues of multiple bond type are shown i.e. stacking of lines.
