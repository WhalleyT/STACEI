Peptide electron density validation

These visualisations allow validation of the peptide held within the MHC groove. Electron density maps from which the peptide atomic positions have been modelled are shown to indicate experimental evidence of their locale. Omit map analysis allows visualisation of experimental electron density data after the omission of peptide atoms and thus can be used to ensure lack of model bias during refinement.
